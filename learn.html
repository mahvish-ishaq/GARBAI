Tips for Effective Waste Disposal

  • Segregate waste at the source into organic, recyclable, and hazardous.
  • Use biodegradable bags for organic waste.
  • Clean recyclable items before disposal to reduce contamination.

Understanding Waste Categories

Waste is typically divided into four categories:

  • Organic: Food scraps, garden waste.
  • Recyclable: Plastics, paper, metal, glass.
  • Hazardous: Batteries, chemicals, electronic waste.
  • Electronic: Old gadgets, cables, and appliances.

The Importance of Waste Segregation

Proper segregation ensures efficient recycling, reduces landfill use, and promotes environmental sustainability.
